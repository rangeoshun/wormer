WELCOME TO WROMIA!

The super fun and awesome self-closing open world, that is like snake, but super fun and awesome.

You can control your worm with [UP], [DOWN], [LEFT] and [RIGHT].

You can use [SPACE] to utilize special ability, obtained by eating up strange points on the field.

The dead have the special ability to respawn, so in the highly likely case of dying, use [SPACE] to respawn.

Gain points by eating certain dots, or by killing and dismembering your fellow worms.

Become immortal by getting on the scoreboard on the right.
